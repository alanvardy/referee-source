all about seminars

The lynchpin of becoming a referee is completion of a seminar. Depending on what level referee you're eligible of becoming, you'll need to complete a seminar with an on-ice session, or a seminar with only classroom time. If you're like me you wish every seminar included an on-ice session, but alas, some refs get jipped during registration.
